Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Vikas Kumar, and I am a dedicated and experienced web developer specializing in front-end development. I am excited to share my skills, experiences, and achievements with you.


From an early age, I have been fascinated by the world of web development. What started as a curiosity quickly turned into a deep passion, and I have since dedicated myself to honing my skills and staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends.


I am proficient in various stages of the development cycle for dynamic web projects. My expertise lies in utilizing technologies such as React.js, SharePoint Framework, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and Adobe Photoshop to create clean interfaces, intuitive interactions, and exceptional user experiences.


Collaboration is key to my work philosophy, and I thoroughly enjoy working alongside designers to bring their vision to life. I thrive in managing large and complex design projects for corporate clients, working closely with senior developers to ensure optimal workflow and project success.


Throughout my career, I have developed a strong background in project management and customer relations. I understand the importance of delivering high-quality work within deadlines, and I am skilled at conducting quality assurance tests to optimize usability and identify any potential errors.


In terms of education, I hold a Post Graduation Diploma in Computer Application from MRS College Malout, Panjab University, Chandigarh, and a Bachelor of Science degree from DAV College Abohar, Panjab University, Chandigarh.


I am proud to have received the "Star of Quarter 1 2022" Achievers Award for my exceptional performance and dedication. After eight months of hard work, I was also rewarded with a significant hike, which further motivated me to strive for excellence.


Beyond my professional endeavors, I have a passion for Bhangra, photography, and reading books. These activities provide me with a well-rounded perspective and inspire creativity in my work.
